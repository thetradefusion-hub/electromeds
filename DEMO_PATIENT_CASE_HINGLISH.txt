DEMO PATIENT CASE - HINGLISH (For NLP Testing)
================================================

Patient Case Description:

Mujhe bahut anxiety hoti hai, especially subah 5 baje ke baad. Main bahut restless rehta hoon aur thand bilkul bardasht nahi hoti. Headache right side me hota hai jo morning me worse hota hai. Mera appetite kam hai aur mujhe kuch bhi khane ka mann nahi karta. Thirst bhi bahut kam hai, din bhar ek do glass pani bhi nahi pita. Sleep bhi theek nahi hai, raat ko 2-3 baje tak neend nahi aati aur jab aati hai to bahut restless sleep hoti hai. Cold weather me meri sabhi symptoms aur badh jati hain. Motion se bhi dard badhta hai. Main akela rehna prefer karta hoon, company se irritate ho jata hoon. Mujhe death ka bahut dar lagta hai aur anxiety attacks aate hain jo sudden start hote hain.
